About Academic ChatAssist

Your AI-powered assistant for academic information in AMC

Our Mission

AMC Academic ChatAssist was created to streamline access to institutional information and provide instant answers to academic queries. Our mission is to enhance the educational experience by making information readily accessible to all staff and students.

How It Works

Our ChatAssist uses advanced AI technology powered by state-of-the-art language models and retrieval augmented generation (RAG) to provide accurate and contextual responses to your questions. The system maintains separate knowledge bases for different academic domains, ensuring you get the most relevant information.
